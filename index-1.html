What to expect?

Skyosiri is located just a short drive from Bangalore, making it a convenient escape into nature. Whether you’re planning a day trip, a weekend getaway, or a corporate event, we welcome you to experience all that Skyosiri has to offer.

Need a Break? Find Your Peace Here

At Skyosiri, unwind amidst the serene beauty of the countryside. From tranquil farm stays to rejuvenating yoga sessions, we provide the perfect environment for relaxation. Immerse yourself in the calm of nature, enjoy peaceful walks, or simply relax with a book while listening to the soothing sounds of rural life. Whether it’s a personal retreat or a relaxing family escape, Skyosiri offers you the peace of mind you’ve been searching for.

Ready for an Adventure? Let’s Explore!

For the thrill-seekers, Skyosiri combines the beauty of nature with exciting activities. Explore the farm with guided treks, enjoy cycling along scenic trails, or challenge yourself with outdoor games. Dive into the hands-on experience of sustainable farming and learn new skills in a fun, interactive way. Skyosiri’s adventure experiences are perfect for families, friends, and corporate teams looking to bond and make lasting memories.

Love Nature? Connect with the Outdoors

Skyosiri is a haven for nature enthusiasts. Discover the beauty of diverse flora and fauna on our eco-trails, learn about organic farming, and connect with nature like never before. From birdwatching to experiencing the life cycle of crops, every visit to Skyosiri is an opportunity to deepen your appreciation for the natural world. Come explore, learn, and leave with a renewed respect for the environment.
